How to identify phishing links

  • Check the sender's email address carefully. Legitimate emails are from official domains.
  • Hover over links to see their destination before clicking.
  • Look for misspellings or generic greetings like "Dear User."
  • Never provide sensitive information like passwords through email.
  • Be cautious of urgent requests or offers that seem too good to be true.

Risks of Falling for Phishing Attacks

  1. Compromise of personal or financial information.
  2. Unauthorized access to your accounts.
  3. Spread of malware or ransomware.

Stay vigilant and report suspicious emails to your IT department or service provider.
